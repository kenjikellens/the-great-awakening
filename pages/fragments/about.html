About The Project

General data transparency & public record preservation.

Our Mission

The Great Awakening is an independent research collective providing a central, stable repository for institutional reports, public records, and verified source material.

We believe that General Data Transparency is the cornerstone of a functioning society. By presenting raw data without editorial bias, we empower citizens to draw conclusions based on factual evidence.

Methodology

  • Primary Sourcing: Built upon original documents and official transcripts.
  • Immutable Archival: Records maintained in their original form to prevent revisionism.
  • Cross-Verification: Triangulated across multiple datasets for accuracy.
  • Public Accessibility: Released under open-access principles for all.
